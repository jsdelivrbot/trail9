Typography

Apollo Typography

Typography uses font size, color and weight to create clear hierarchy, creating a balanced delivery of information. Typography can be leveraged to articulate hierarchy, with the execution of weight, size and color all playing a factor. The absence of hierarchy on a page can have negative effects by forcing a consumer to exert more-than-desired effort in order to consume the content. Proper balance will always give a clear, stable progressive flow for consumption.

Font Selections

Proxima Nova Light is our primary typeface. If using Proxima Nova at sizes smaller than 10 point use Proxima Nova Book. For print and marketing materials, Proxima Nova is used primarily for display and title case scenarios. User interfaces utilize Proxima Nova exclusively. Merriweather Light is our secondary typeface, used for body copy and large areas of non-interactive text.
